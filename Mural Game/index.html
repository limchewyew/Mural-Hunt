Kampung Gelam Mural Hunt

How to play

  1. Explore the map and tap on each mural or attraction pin to reveal a hint.
  2. Type your guess for each location in the popup textbox.
  3. If your guess is correct, the answer will be revealed and locked in for that mural or attraction.
  4. Each wrong guess adds a part to the hangman. Complete the hangman and you'll have to restart!
  5. Find all the correct answers to win and celebrate your success!

Have fun discovering Kampung Gelam and good luck on your mural hunt!
