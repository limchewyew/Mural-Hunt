Kampung Gelam Mural Hunt

How to play

  1. Explore the map and tap on each attraction pin to reveal details.
  2. For regular attractions (blue pins): Type your guess in the textbox. If correct, the answer will be revealed and locked in.
  3. For special attractions (red pins): Take a wefie or photo of the location as instructed.
  4. Each wrong guess on regular attractions adds a part to the hangman. Complete the hangman and you'll have to restart!
  5. Complete all guessing challenges AND take photos of all 3 special locations in the fastest time among all teams to win!

Race against other teams to discover Kampung Glam and complete all challenges first!
